About Us

Welcome to Sri Devi Bhavan, a culinary legacy in Devanahalli since 1953. We pride ourselves on authentic South Indian flavors passed down through generations, served with love on banana leaves alongside aromatic filter coffee. Our mission is to keep traditions alive, offering a warm, family-like experience with every meal.
